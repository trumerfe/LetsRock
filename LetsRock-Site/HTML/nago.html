Nagoriyuki

A skilled samurai who is also a member of the nightwalkers, a vampire-like race of humans. Happy-Chaos continued to use binding magic on him to force him as his bodyguard and to distract Sol and the others. With every blade clash with Sol, he continued to gain more control until he eventually broke free and let sol strike him, proving his free will from Happy Chaos. Once he healed up, he immediately joined Sol, Jack-O, and Ky in the final fight. His help was crucial in defeating the empowered godlike I-No and ending Happy Chaos' plans. His current whereabouts are unknown.
