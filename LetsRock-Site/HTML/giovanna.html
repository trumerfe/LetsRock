Giovanna

An officer tasked with helping to protect the President of the United States, Giovanna is highly skilled in close quarters combat. Joined by her spirit wolf Rei, the two put on a brillant display of combining their skills to overpower their opponents. She was crucial in helping Sol and the others defeat Hppy Chaos and I-No, but was injured in the going battle. She is currently recovering from her injuries.
